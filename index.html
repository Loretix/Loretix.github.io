Spotivibes

A web application where you can create your "vibes" by putting on the map the songs that remind you of each of your favorite places. Make playlists and share them with your friends.

Spotivibes is a project carried out within the framework of the Web Systems and Technologies subject at the University of Zaragoza in collaboration with my classmates Jaime Roncal, Pilar Ester, and Ignacio Ortega. It is not yet public, so to enjoy it, it is necessary to request access by writing an email to "contacto.spotivibes@gmail.com".
