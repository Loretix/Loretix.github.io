Spotivibes is a project for the Web Systems and Technologies course in the Computer Science degree programme at the University of Zaragoza. It involves working in teams to develop a responsive web application.
The website developed is a kind of social network that allows users to place songs on a map and share them with friends. The song placed on the map is called a vibe, as it is associated with a place and will remind you of something that happened to you there.
